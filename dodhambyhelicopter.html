Kedarnath Darshan

Upon arrival at the Sahastradhara helipad, our representative will welcome you and escort you to your helicopter. From there you’ll be taken to sirsi, en route you will be treated with breathtaking view of mountains and gentle rolling hills.

Meanwhile, when you’ll be amusing with magic of snow and divine beauty our helicopter will take you to the Kedarnath Now clasp your hands and bow your head as you have reached on the land of Kedar and you can straight away proceed for the VIP darshan at Kedarnath temple. Badrinath Darshan

Second half of your day will captivate your heart as you are heading towards a beauteous destination, Badrinath.

Feel the cool breeze and soothing chants of priest as you embark into the Badrinath temple for VIP darshan. Be dazzled by the breathtaking sights of Badrinath and you’ll be transferred to hotel Sarovar Portico for delectable lunch.

With this specially designed Badrinath helicopter tour, enjoy the much needed family- time in the divine lap of nature. After darshan, leave for S’dhara helipad.
